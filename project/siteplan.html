Site Name

Tro Opong Samuel - Web & Programming Portfolio

Domain Suggestion: TroEJS.dev

Why this name?

The name represents my personal brand as a web developer and programmer, showcasing my skills, projects, and availability for work.

Site Purpose

This website serves as a professional portfolio and a point of contact for potential clients and employers.

  • Showcase my **experience and technical skills**
  • Display a **portfolio of completed projects**
  • Provide a **resume and certifications** for hiring managers
  • Offer a **blog** with programming tips, tutorials, and insights
  • Include a **testimonial section** from past clients or colleagues
  • Have a **contact page** for direct inquiries

Scenarios

1. **A client looking for a freelance web developer**

How can I see examples of your previous web development projects, and what services do you offer?

2. **A company interested in hiring me full-time**

Can I view your resume and learn more about your professional background and skills?

3. **A beginner developer seeking mentorship**

Do you have any blog posts or resources to help me learn web development?

4. **A recruiter looking for skilled developers**

Do you have experience with front-end and back-end technologies? What frameworks do you specialize in?
